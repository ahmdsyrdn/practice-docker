About Us

RAMATA is an anonymous aspirational website dedicated to universities. RAMATA was created in October 2022 by four UGM students, namely Ahmad Syahruddin, Josua Rusdi, Kinar Nakeisha, and Lasyitha Azzahra. Our website is intended for students and the academic community by providing aspirations with certain categories anonymously. We protect the identity of the sender and get an honest evaluation in the form of criticism and suggestions. Users will provide honest criticism and suggestions without fear of interference from anyone. We are aware of the problem and improve the quality of service. Therefore, the evaluation can be carried out by universities based on the RAMATA website.
